Definition of Unschooling

Unschooling is a learner-led educational philosophy also known as self-directed learning. It is an ideological return to life-long learning and learning naturally from playing, experiencing life and coming up against challenges and mistakes that have no prescribed solution.

Unschooling vs Homeschooling

Homeschooling: Let's clear up any confusion. Homeschooling is more like 1:1 school at home. Testing, reporting with standardized, purchased or self-created curriculum. Children generally don't have a say. Subjects remain separated and there is a clear grade level progression.

Expressed at varied levels of strictness there is no one size fits all, however it is generally accepted that homeschooling while a broad term for "we are not enrolled in an institution for education, as methodology means learning largely looks structured and/or graded.
