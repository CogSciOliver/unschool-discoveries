## Short Bio

Danii Oliver is the creator of **Unschool Discoveries**, a story-world project centered on curiosity, self-directed learning, and the lived realities of unschooling families. Working across storytelling, publishing, and digital worldbuilding, Oliver creates books and companion media that help children see learning through living, exploration, play, and real experience rather than classroom confinement.

## Standard Bio

Danii Oliver is the creator of **Unschool Discoveries**, a story-world and publishing project created for curious families and self-directed learners. Through books, companion media, and an expanding publication ecosystem, Oliver explores what it looks like for children to learn through life, play, exploration, and real-world experience.

At the center of the project is **Unschool Discoveries: Venturing into the Unknown**, part of a larger effort to create story-rich cultural representation for unschoolers and families living beyond conventional education models. The work extends into **Unschool Pages**, the living publication platform connected to the universe, where readers can explore notebook-style expansions, rabbit holes, field notes, and behind-the-scenes layers of the project.

Oliver’s work brings together storytelling, authorship, digital publishing, and worldbuilding to create media that feels imaginative, reflective, and grounded in authentic learning. The broader goal is not simply to publish books, but to build a universe where curiosity, autonomy, and unconventional learning are treated as worthy of beauty, story, and serious creative attention.

## Extended Bio

Danii Oliver is an author, builder, and creator of **Unschool Discoveries**, a story-world project designed to give unschooling and self-directed learning families something they have rarely been offered: story-based cultural representation rooted in real learning, real curiosity, and real life.

Through books, companion materials, and a growing digital publication ecosystem, Oliver’s work centers children who learn by living. Rather than presenting learning as something confined to classrooms, curriculum, or compliance, **Unschool Discoveries** presents childhood as a space of exploration, agency, play, experimentation, and discovery. The project invites readers into a world where curiosity is not a side note but the engine of the story.

Oliver is also the force behind **Unschool Pages**, the living publication platform connected to the broader Unschool Discoveries universe. There, the project expands beyond the book itself into notebook-style writing, rabbit holes, field notes, behind-the-scenes materials, and deeper thematic explorations. Together, the two platforms create a layered reading experience: one rooted in story-world immersion, the other in living publication and expansion.

The creative vision behind Oliver’s work is both literary and structural. It is not only about writing books, but about building a sustained universe around ideas of self-direction, wonder, autonomy, and meaningful learning. In a cultural landscape where unschooling families are often flattened, misunderstood, or ignored, Oliver’s work aims to create something more enduring: a world where these families can feel seen, reflected, and imaginatively at home.
