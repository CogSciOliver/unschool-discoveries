## What Is Unschool Discoveries?

**Unschool Discoveries** is a story-world for curious families.

It was created for children and families who learn through life, play, questions, adventure, and everyday discovery.

## Why It Exists

Many children’s books still treat school as the center of childhood.

**Unschool Discoveries** was created to make room for something different.

It makes room for curiosity.  
It makes room for freedom.  
It makes room for children who learn by living.

## Who It Is For

Unschool Discoveries is for:

- curious kids
- unschooling families
- self-directed learners
- families who want stories that feel more like real life

## What Makes It Different

This is not a school-centered story project.

It is built around wonder, exploration, and the many ways learning can happen outside the classroom.

It is meant to help children feel seen and to help families find stories that reflect the way they actually live and learn.

## The Bigger World

**Unschool Discoveries** is the story-world front door.

**Unschool Pages** is the connected living publication platform where the world grows through notes, rabbit holes, behind-the-scenes posts, and deeper reflections.

Together, they create a larger universe of story, curiosity, and discovery.

## In One Line

**Unschool Discoveries** is a story-world for curious families that celebrates wonder, freedom, and learning through real life.
