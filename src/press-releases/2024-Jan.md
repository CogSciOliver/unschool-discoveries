# Unschool Discoveries Officially Releases *Venturing into the Unknown*

**A new story for curious families is now here.**

**[Dallas/Fort Worth, Texas] — [Janurary 04, 2024]** — **Danii Oliver** has officially released *Venturing into the Unknown*, the first book in the Unschool Discoveries series.

Created for curious families, unschoolers, and self-directed learners, *Venturing into the Unknown* invites readers into a story-world shaped by wonder, exploration, and learning through real life. The book centers children who learn by living, playing, asking questions, and discovering the world around them.

“This book was created to give families something they do not often get to see,” said Danii Oliver, creator of **Unschool Discoveries**. “It makes room for children whose lives are full of curiosity, freedom, and real learning.”

*Venturing into the Unknown* is part of the growing **Unschool Discoveries** universe. Readers can also explore **Unschool Pages**, the connected living publication platform that expands the world through notes, rabbit holes, and behind-the-scenes discoveries.

With this official release, **Unschool Discoveries** continues its mission to create stories that help children feel seen, inspired, and proud of the many ways learning can happen.

More information, media resources, and project updates are available on the official **Unschool Discoveries** website.

## About Unschool Discoveries

**Unschool Discoveries** is a story-world project for curious families, unschoolers, and self-directed learners. Through books, media, and a growing publication platform, the project celebrates wonder, freedom, and learning through real life.
