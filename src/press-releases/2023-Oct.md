# Unschool Discoveries Announces *Venturing into the Unknown* Now in Production

**A new story-world for curious families and self-directed learners moves one step closer to release.**

**[Dallas/Fort Worth, Texas] — [October 04, 2023]** — **Danii Olivers** has announced that their first book, *Venturing into the Unknown*, is now in production.

Created for curious families, unschoolers, and self-directed learners, *Venturing into the Unknown* is part of a larger story-world project built around wonder, freedom, and learning through real life. The book is designed to give children and families something they do not often see in books: stories that reflect learning through play, exploration, questions, movement, and everyday discovery.

“Too often, children’s books treat school as the center of childhood,” said Danii Oliver, creator of **Unschool Discoveries**. “This project was created to make room for something else — a story-world where curiosity, real-life learning, and self-direction are part of the adventure.”

The book is part of the growing **Unschool Discoveries** universe, which also connects to **Unschool Pages**, the living publication platform where readers can explore deeper themes, notes, rabbit holes, and behind-the-scenes development connected to the project.

With *Venturing into the Unknown* now in production, **Unschool Discoveries** is moving toward the next phase of bringing the project to readers, families, and media audiences interested in stories shaped by creativity, autonomy, and discovery.

More updates, release news, and media information will be shared through the official **Unschool Discoveries** website.

## About Unschool Discoveries

**Unschool Discoveries** is a story-world project for curious families, unschoolers, and self-directed learners. Through books, media, and a growing publication ecosystem, the project celebrates wonder, freedom, and learning through real life.
